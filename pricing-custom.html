Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about custom solutions.

How long does a custom solution take?

It depends on complexity. Simple customizations (branding, basic integrations) can be done in 2-4 weeks. Complex projects with multiple integrations and custom model training typically take 6-12 weeks.

How much does a custom solution cost?

Custom solutions start at around €1,000/month plus a one-time setup fee. The exact cost depends on your requirements, integrations, and volume. Tell us about your needs and we’ll send a detailed quote.

Do you offer on-premise deployment?

Yes, for enterprise customers we support deployment on your own infrastructure or private cloud. This is ideal for organizations with strict data residency or security requirements.

Can I start with a standard plan and upgrade later?

Absolutely. Many customers start with a standard plan and then move to custom as their needs grow. We’ll work with you to design a solution that scales with your business.

What kind of integrations do you support?

Nearly anything with an API. Common integrations include Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, booking systems, ERP software, and internal tools. If your system has an API, we can connect to it.

Is there a pilot option for custom solutions?

Yes! We offer pilot programs for custom solutions too. You can test a scoped version of your custom setup before committing to a full rollout.
