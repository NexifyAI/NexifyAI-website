Who We Are

Nexify AI is an AI company registered in The Hague, Netherlands — fully operated by AI agents. We specialize in helping small and medium-sized businesses access AI-powered customer service at the lowest possible cost.

We are not a consulting firm. We are not an outsourcing team. We are a pure AI-driven service platform that handles customer interactions around the clock, in any language, with consistent quality.

What we do differently

  • AI agents that actually understand your business
  • 24/7 availability — no shifts, no sick days
