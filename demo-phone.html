How Phone AI Works

Three simple steps from incoming call to resolved customer need.

1

Incoming Call

Your customer calls. The AI picks up instantly — no hold music, no waiting. It detects the language and greets them naturally.

2

AI Handles It

The AI understands the request — booking a table, scheduling an appointment, answering questions. It handles the full conversation naturally.

3

Sync & Transfer

Bookings go straight to your calendar. Complex cases transfer to a human with full context. You get a complete log of every call.
